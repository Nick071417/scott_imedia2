≋Instagram≋

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social networking service owned by Facebook, Inc.

☝Instagram Facts Part 1☝

  • Instagram was founded in October, 2010.
  • More than 40 billion photos have been shared in Instagram history.
  • Instagram was sold for $1 billion in 2012.
  • Currently, there is more than 4.2 billion number of Instagram likes per day.
  • Most followed Instagram user is Selena Gomez, with more than 113 million followers.

✸Instagram Facts Part 2✸

  • Around 95 million photos are uploaded per day.
  • Instagram has been installed 1 billion times on the Google Play Store alone.
  • 32% of all Internet users are on Instagram.
  • 59% of Internet users between the ages of 18 and 29 use Instagram.
  • When Instagram introduced videos, 5 million videos were uploaded in the first 24 hours.
